Append a new entry to `docs/session-log.md` (newest at the top, after the template block) covering this session.

Use today's date and include:

- **What we did** — bullet list, specific file/template names
- **Files changed** — list each with what changed (theme files, brief files, metafields created, etc.)
- **Decisions made** — and the reasoning, briefly
- **Open questions** — anything blocked or undecided
- **Next session: start here** — the single first thing to pick up

Keep it dense and specific. Future-you will thank present-you for naming files and line numbers instead of writing "updated some homepage stuff."

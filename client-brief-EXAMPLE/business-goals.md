# Business Goals & Context — Lupine Field Coffee

## About the Business
**Industry/niche:** Specialty single-origin coffee roaster
**Years in business:** 4 years (founded 2022)
**Current revenue stage:** Early growth — $280k/yr, 60% wholesale to cafés, 40% DTC
**Primary sales channel today:** Wholesale + farmers market; DTC site exists but underperforming

## Goals for This Store

**Primary goal:** Grow DTC revenue from 40% to 60% of total within 12 months. Specifically, drive subscription sign-ups — the quarterly "Field Notes" subscription is the highest-margin product and locks in repeat revenue.

**Secondary goals:**
- Build email list to 5,000 (currently ~800) so we have a marketing channel that doesn't depend on Instagram
- Reduce time merchant spends on order fulfillment per week (currently ~6 hrs of admin)
- Establish the brand as the "thoughtful person's coffee" in the Mountain West market

## Target Customer

**Who is the ideal buyer?** 32–55, lives in the Mountain West or PNW, household income $80k+, drinks coffee daily, follows at least one specialty coffee account on Instagram, owns a pour-over setup or aspires to. Reads. Hikes. Cares where things come from.

**What problem are they solving?** They're tired of grocery store coffee but find most third-wave coffee marketing pretentious. They want coffee that's clearly better and a brand that respects their intelligence. They will pay $22/lb if they trust the source.

**Where do they come from?** Instagram (organic, not ads — ~45%), word-of-mouth from cafés that serve our coffee (~25%), Google search for "Montana coffee roaster" or specific origin terms (~20%), other (~10%).

## Conversion Priorities

1. **Subscription sign-up** — quarterly recurring, highest LTV
2. **Email signup** — the gateway, especially for non-subscribers
3. **First-time bag purchase** — proof point that converts to subscription later

## Products / Catalog

**Number of products:** 18 (12 active coffees + 4 pieces of brew gear + 2 subscriptions)
**SKU complexity:** Variants — coffee has whole-bean / ground options (4 grind sizes) and 12oz / 5lb sizes
**Average order value (target):** $48 (currently $34 — driven up by subscription bundling)
**Key products to feature:** Field Notes Subscription, Highline Blend (best-seller), the seasonal single-origin we have on hand

## Competitive Landscape

**Main competitors:** Drift Coffee (Whitefish), Black Coffee Roasters (Missoula), and the national "thoughtful" players (Heart, Onyx, Sey). We don't compete with grocery brands.

**Our differentiator:** We publish the actual purchase price of every green bean and the markup. No other Mountain West roaster does this. The merchant is also a former NPR journalist — the brand voice is genuinely different from "coffee dude" voice.

## Known Constraints

- **Budget:** No budget for paid apps over $30/mo. Subscription app is non-negotiable (Recharge, already installed).
- **Timeline:** Soft target of 6 weeks for the homepage + product page rebuild before holiday season.
- **Technical:** Merchant is non-technical. Theme edits must be reversible. No headless / Hydrogen.
- **Must-have apps:** Recharge (subscriptions), Klaviyo (email — already installed).
- **No discounting** — brand position is "fair price always," so don't propose sale banners or BOGO mechanics.
